About Me

Hey! I’m HayZr, a 16-year-old music producer from the Netherlands. I make electronic music — mostly house, techno, and EDM — but honestly, I listen to all kinds of music. Producing started as a hobby and it still is, but I take it seriously and hope to grow into something bigger.

I’m still studying, so I work on music in between school and life. My first track “Tududu” is coming soon, and I really hope you enjoy it — and all the new songs I’m working on. Thanks for checking out my music. Let's see where this journey goes 🚀
